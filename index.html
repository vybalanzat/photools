Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the beginning of each game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points on your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of the crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal
